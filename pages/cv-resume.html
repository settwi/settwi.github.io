About

William is a 3rd year graduate student at University of Minnesota, Twin Cities. He works on X-ray instrumentation and analysis of X-ray and other data to investigate solar flare particle acceleration. William has a passion for self-learning and has pursued many personal projects. He passed his written exam at the end of his first year, in May 2021. He passed his oral exam at the end of his second year, in June 2022.
